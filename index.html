About Sky Observation

Are you an astronomy enthusiast? 🧚‍♀️🧚‍♀️🧚‍♀️ Sky Observation lets you log what you see in the night sky: nebulae, galaxies, planets, comets, lunar features. Each entry records the object's right ascension and declination, the location you observed from, and a free-form description. You can search the community observations, build personal collections you can print as PDFs, comment on other people's observations, and rate them.
